ABOUT ME

I am a former Olympic athlete and accomplished full-stack software developer specializing in high-performance and scalable applications. My work blends software engineering, modern web development, and refined visual design to deliver clean, efficient, and reliable technical solutions.

I hold a BS in Software Development from the University of Utah and bring strong proficiency in Java, React, Python, SQL, Kotlin, PHP, Node.js, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I am recognized for producing well architected systems, precise technical documentation, and optimized, maintainable codebases.

I served as the Olympic Technician Manager for U.S. Speedskating, where I led technical operations and performance optimization for national and Olympic teams. I am also a 2018 Winter Olympic athlete and an internationally recognized classical guitarist, with performances featured at Carnegie Hall, NPR, and PBS, demonstrating the discipline, creativity, and execution standards I bring into software engineering.
