About Me

I am a retired Olympic Athlete turned full-stack software developer. I am passionate about software engineering, web development, and graphic design. I thrive on designing and implementing efficient technical solutions and attention to detail.

I hold a BS in Software Development from the University of Utah (2026). I am skilled in Java, C++, C#, Python, SQL, PHP, JavaScript, Django, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, as well as technical documentation and application optimization.

My experience spans serving as the Equipment Technician Manager for US Speedskating, providing technical expertise and program optimization for National and Olympic teams. Additionally, I am a former Olympic athlete (2018 Winter Games, South Korea) and an internationally renowned classical guitarist (Carnegie Hall, NPR, PBS).
